Championing Sustainable Solutions: SoftXchange Tech Joins Forces with the Environmental Markets Association

Aug 30, 2023 | Reading Time: 2 minutes

We're Thrilled to Join the Environmental Markets Association (EMA) in Advancing Sustainable Solutions. SoftXchange's Commitment to Innovation in Environmental Markets Reinforced. SoftXchange is excited to announce its membership in the Environmental Markets Association (EMA). This partnership underscores our unwavering commitment to pioneering innovative exchange platforms within the realm of environmental markets. As active EMA members, we look forward to collaborating with industry leaders in the dynamic landscape of environmental markets, encompassing carbon offsets, alternative energy trading, and emerging asset classes. Through this collaboration, SoftXchange aims to craft tailored solutions that effectively address the distinctive challenges faced by this industry, ultimately driving sustainability and positive change.

Exploring the Environmental Markets Association (EMA)

EMA serves as a dynamic nexus for collaborative endeavors, uniting a diverse spectrum of perspectives with a common goal: the effective implementation of market-driven solutions to benefit our environment. Comprising members from environmental asset brokers, exchanges, large utilities, law firms, project developers, consultants, academics, NGOs, and government agencies, EMA operates as a central hub for knowledge sharing, expertise exchange, and collective advocacy, fostering industry-wide progress. Crucially, EMA also takes on the vital role of vigilant oversight, meticulously monitoring legislative and regulatory developments impacting environmental markets, ensuring their unwavering commitment to the highest standards of environmental responsibility.

Turning Insights into Environmental Innovation

With a distinguished history of successfully delivering EP3, an exchange matching engine and platform, across diverse global markets, our collaboration with EMA reinforces our mission to usher in cutting-edge markets and enhance infrastructure for existing ones to evolve alongside the industry. At SoftXchange, our forte lies in crafting scalable, reliable, and adaptable exchange platforms, empowering marketplaces to operate with confidence and convenience. Through our EMA membership, we bolster our capacity to directly engage with market participants, ensuring EP3 remains at the forefront of functionality, delivering comprehensive solutions in line with the highest standards of market integrity within environmental markets. "SoftXchange has always been a leader in developing new markets, so it was an obvious decision for us to join the Environmental Markets Association given our prior experience with environmental market technology and the ongoing industry evolution," remarked SoftXchange's Head of Growth, Don David.
